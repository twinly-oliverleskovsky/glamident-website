# GlamiDent Color Scheme Analysis

## Primary Colors

### Gold/Yellow Accent
- **Primary CTA Button**: `#D4A900` or similar (bright gold/yellow)
- Used on: "OBJEDNAŤ SA" buttons throughout the site
- This is the most prominent action color

### Olive Green / Dark Olive
- **Primary Heading Color**: Olive green (approximately `#6B7C3F` or `#7A8B4A`)
- Used on: Major headings like "Sme tu pre váš zdravý úsmev", "Recenzie", "Prijímame nových pacientov!"
- **Secondary Buttons**: Darker olive/green shade
- Used on: "OBJEDNAŤ SA" buttons in content sections, "ZOBRAZIŤ VŠETKY" buttons
- Appears to be around `#5F6F3D` to `#6B7C40`

### Dark Background
- **Navigation Bar**: Very dark charcoal/black `#2B2B2B` or `#333333`
- **Footer**: Same dark charcoal color
- Creates strong contrast with lighter content areas

## Secondary Colors

### White
- **Primary Background**: Pure white `#FFFFFF`
- Used for: Main content areas, cards, sections
- Creates clean, medical/dental feel

### Light Gray
- **Background Variations**: Very light gray for subtle section separation
- Approximately `#F5F5F5` or `#F8F8F8`
- Used in: Reviews section background, some content cards

### Gold Outline/Border
- **Navigation Active State**: Gold/yellow border `#D4A900`
- Used on: Active "DOMOV" link in navigation

### Beige/Tan
- **News/Article Buttons**: Light tan/beige color
- Approximately `#C9A966` or similar
- Used on: "VŠETKY NOVINKY" button

## Text Colors

### Primary Text
- **Dark Charcoal**: `#333333` or `#2B2B2B`
- Used for: Body text, descriptions, main content

### White Text
- **On Dark Backgrounds**: Pure white `#FFFFFF`
- Used on: Navigation items, footer text, hero section text
- Used on: Gold buttons ("OBJEDNAŤ SA")

### Olive Green Text
- **Headings**: Same olive green as primary color
- **Links**: "ZISTIŤ VIAC", "CENNÍK" links in gold/yellow

### Gold/Yellow Text
- **Secondary Links**: `#D4A900` or similar
- Used on: "VIAC O KLINIKE →", "sociálnych sieťach" link

## Accent & Brand Colors

### Teal/Turquoise Icon
- **Service Icons**: Light teal/turquoise for tooth and lip icons
- Approximately `#4DB8B8` or `#5CC5C5`
- Used for: Dental and aesthetic medicine service icons

### Green (Various Shades)
- **3D Tour Section**: Dark olive green background
- **Content Boxes**: Olive/green backgrounds for emphasis sections
- Range from `#5F6F3D` to `#7A8B4A`

## Image & Photography Style

### Hero Section
- Dark botanical background (dark green monstera leaves)
- Creates sophisticated, natural aesthetic
- Team photo: White pants, black uniforms with GlamiDent branding

### Clinical Photos
- Bright, white clinic interiors
- Olive green seating (matching brand color)
- Large windows with natural light
- Modern, clean aesthetic

## Button States & Styles

### Primary CTA (Call-to-Action)
- Background: Bright gold `#D4A900`
- Text: White `#FFFFFF`
- Style: Rectangle with subtle border
- Used for: Main "OBJEDNAŤ SA" buttons

### Secondary Buttons
- Background: Olive green `#5F6F3D` to `#6B7C40`
- Text: White or light yellow
- Style: Rectangle, similar to primary

### Text Links
- Color: Gold/yellow `#D4A900` or olive green
- Hover: Likely underline or color shift

## Brand Identity Summary

**Core Brand Colors:**
1. **Gold Yellow**: `#D4A900` - Energy, optimism, premium service
2. **Olive Green**: `#6B7C3F` - Natural, health, calm, professional
3. **Dark Charcoal**: `#2B2B2B` - Sophistication, professionalism
4. **White**: `#FFFFFF` - Cleanliness, medical, trust

**Supporting Colors:**
- Teal/Turquoise: `#4DB8B8` - Freshness, medical
- Light Beige/Tan: `#C9A966` - Warmth, approachable
- Light Gray: `#F5F5F5` - Subtle backgrounds

## Design Philosophy

The color scheme combines:
- **Medical/Clinical**: White backgrounds, clean layouts
- **Natural/Organic**: Olive greens, botanical imagery
- **Premium/Luxury**: Gold accents, dark sophisticated tones
- **Modern**: Strong contrast, bold typography, clean sections

This creates a brand identity that feels:
- Professional and trustworthy (medical whites and dark tones)
- Natural and calming (green tones, plant imagery)
- Premium and high-quality (gold accents)
- Modern and sophisticated (clean design, strong hierarchy)
